Getting Started

Select the page you want to edit and start designing your website with an intuitive and powerful editor. Take full control of your content structure and layout.

SEO Optimization

Enhance your site's visibility by managing Search Engine Optimization (SEO) settings effortlessly. Customize meta titles, descriptions, and keywords to improve search engine ranking.

Customization & Design

Explore a variety of design tools to personalize your website. Drag and drop elements, modify styles, and preview your changes in real time to create a professional-looking page with ease.

Editing Text

Modify text directly on your page by simply clicking on any text element. Use the text editor to apply styles, change fonts, adjust alignment, and even add links or lists in a few clicks.

Adding & Editing Images

Drag an image block into your section, then double-click it to open the image options. You can:

  • 📂 Upload an image from your computer by clicking twice.
  • 🌐 Paste an image URL in the provided field to load it from the web.
  • 🛠️ Adjust the size and alignment using the style panel.

Exploring Available Blocks

GrapesJS provides a variety of drag-and-drop blocks to customize your layout. Click the "Blocks" panel to explore and choose from elements like:

  • 📄 Text & Heading - Add and edit text-based content.
  • 🖼️ Images & Videos - Enhance your site with media content.
  • 📌 Buttons & Forms - Create interactive elements.
  • 🛠️ Advanced Components - Sliders, tabs, and more.

Changing Colors and Styles

Customize your page design with the Style Manager in GrapesJS. Select any section and modify its:

  • 🎨 Background Color - Apply solid colors or gradients.
  • 🔤 Text Color & Font - Adjust typography for better readability.
  • 📏 Spacing & Borders - Fine-tune padding, margins, and outlines.

Click the "Style" panel and experiment with different settings to match your brand identity.

Creating a New Page

Need a new page? Use the "Add Page" button to create a new blank canvas. Follow these simple steps:

  1. ➕ Click "Add Page" in the top menu.
  2. 📝 Enter a Page Name and set its URL slug.
  3. 📌 Start Designing by adding sections and blocks.
  4. 💾 Save & Publish when you're ready to go live.

This feature allows you to **expand your site dynamically**, adding as many pages as needed with a fully custom design.
